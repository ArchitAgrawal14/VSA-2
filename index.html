Our Achievements

Discover excellence at Vaibhav Skating Academy, a premier skating institution making waves in the districts surrounding the Satna Region. With a proud legacy, we've honed the skills of over 2000 skaters, achieving a remarkable 29 World Records. Beyond our commitment to sporting excellence, we actively participate in social and government campaigns, emphasizing our broader impact. Every year, our skilled skaters proudly represent Team MP at National Levels, showcasing the academy's dedication to producing top-tier athletes. Embracing diversity, we offer training not only in speed skating but also in roll ball, ice skating, roller hockey and roller basketball, ensuring a comprehensive and enriching experience for all enthusiasts. We are also the doorway for Ice Skating Atheletes in Madhya Pradesh to represent at National Levels. Some of our skaters are National Medalists securing about 1000+ medals overall

Meet our coach

Meet our exceptional coach, Mr. Vaibhav Agrawal, a certified NIS Coach with an impressive 19 years of experience. As a Guinness World Record Holder, his passion for coaching extends beyond boundaries. With a keen eye for detail, he excels in technical precision, fostering a dynamic learning environment. Mr. Agrawal's expertise extends to strategic training methodologies, resulting in the success of over 2000 skaters, many of whom have excelled at both National and International Levels. His commitment to holistic development, coupled with a profound understanding of individual needs, makes him not only an invaluable mentor in the world of skating but also life-changing for a lot of children around Vindhya Region !
